## Discovery call opener
Thanks for taking the call. The goal today is simple: understand how AI is already being used in your business, where the biggest risks or unknowns are, and whether it makes sense to put a lightweight policy and approval structure in place.

## Questions for discovery calls
- Are employees already using AI tools for work today?
- Which tools are showing up most often?
- Are employees using personal accounts or approved company accounts?
- Do you currently have any written AI usage rules?
- Are employees ever using AI for client-facing communication or sensitive documents?
- Who would need to approve an AI use policy internally?
