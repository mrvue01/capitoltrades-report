# AI Use Policy Template, Law Firms

## Purpose
This policy governs responsible use of AI tools in firm operations while protecting confidentiality, privilege, and work quality.

## Allowed uses
- internal brainstorming
- summarizing non-sensitive materials
- drafting internal outlines
- administrative productivity support

## Prohibited uses
- entering privileged or confidential client matter information into unapproved AI tools
- relying on AI output as legal advice without attorney review
- sending AI-generated work product externally without responsible lawyer review

## Human review
Any AI-assisted legal, client-facing, or matter-specific work must be reviewed and approved by a qualified human reviewer before use.

## Note
This policy supports operational governance and does not provide legal ethics advice.
