# AI Risk Assessment Checklist, Law Firms

- [ ] Inventory AI use across attorneys, paralegals, intake, and admin staff
- [ ] Identify use of personal AI accounts
- [ ] Check whether privileged, confidential, or matter-specific content enters AI tools
- [ ] Check whether AI is used for legal drafting or research support
- [ ] Check whether human attorney review is required before external use
- [ ] Check whether approved tools and prohibited uses are defined
- [ ] Check whether confidentiality rules explicitly cover AI
- [ ] Identify high-risk workflows needing immediate restriction or approval
