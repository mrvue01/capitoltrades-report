## Safe AI Use Setup for Pharmacies

Pharmacies and pharmacy-adjacent teams may start using AI for internal drafting, SOP cleanup, staff training content, and administrative workflows. Without rules, staff can expose protected health or patient-related information, rely on unchecked output, or create operational risk.

## What we do
We help pharmacies create practical rules for safe AI use so teams can benefit from AI without mishandling sensitive information or bypassing required human judgment.

- manager approval guidance
- staff training
