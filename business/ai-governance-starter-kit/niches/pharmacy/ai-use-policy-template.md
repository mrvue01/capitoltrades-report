# AI Use Policy Template, Pharmacies

## Purpose
This policy governs employee use of AI tools in pharmacy operations while protecting sensitive information, patient trust, and work quality.

## Allowed uses
- drafting internal notes and SOP language
- summarizing non-sensitive materials
- brainstorming training or communication drafts
- administrative productivity support

## Prohibited uses
- entering patient, prescription, billing, or employee-sensitive data into unapproved AI tools
- relying on AI output for patient-specific, medication-specific, or regulated decisions without qualified human review
- sending AI-generated patient-facing content without appropriate review

## Human review
Any AI-assisted operational, patient-facing, or sensitive workflow output must be reviewed by an assigned qualified human reviewer before use.

## Note
This policy supports operational governance and does not replace HIPAA, legal, clinical, or regulatory advice.
