# AI Risk Assessment Checklist, Pharmacies

- [ ] Inventory AI use across pharmacists, technicians, admin, and operations staff
- [ ] Identify personal AI account use
- [ ] Check whether patient, prescription, billing, or employee data enters AI tools
- [ ] Check whether AI is used for patient-facing communication or clinical-adjacent workflows
- [ ] Check whether pharmacist or manager review is required before use
- [ ] Check whether approved tools and prohibited uses are defined
- [ ] Check whether privacy and operational policies mention AI
- [ ] Identify workflows requiring immediate restriction
