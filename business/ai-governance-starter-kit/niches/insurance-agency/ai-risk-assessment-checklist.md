# AI Risk Assessment Checklist, Insurance Agencies

- [ ] Inventory AI use across producers, service staff, marketing, and admin teams
- [ ] Identify use of personal AI accounts
- [ ] Check whether policyholder, claims, or underwriting data enters AI tools
- [ ] Check whether AI is used for coverage explanations or client-facing communications
- [ ] Check whether manager review is required before client use
- [ ] Check whether approved tools and prohibited uses are defined
- [ ] Check whether privacy/confidentiality rules mention AI use
- [ ] Identify highest-risk workflows needing immediate restrictions
