# AI Use Policy Template, Insurance Agencies

## Purpose
This policy governs employee use of AI tools in agency workflows while protecting policyholder information, internal records, and service quality.

## Allowed uses
- drafting non-sensitive emails
- summarizing internal notes
- brainstorming marketing ideas
- administrative productivity support

## Prohibited uses
- entering policyholder, claims, underwriting, or confidential financial data into unapproved AI tools
- relying on AI output as final coverage guidance without human review
- sending AI-generated client communications without appropriate review

## Human review
AI-assisted client communications, coverage explanations, and sensitive workflow outputs must be reviewed by an assigned human reviewer before use.
