# AI Risk Assessment Checklist, Accounting Firms

- [ ] Inventory AI tools used across tax, bookkeeping, payroll, and advisory teams
- [ ] Identify use of personal or free-tier AI accounts
- [ ] Check whether taxpayer, payroll, banking, or financial statement data is entered into AI tools
- [ ] Check whether AI is used for tax interpretations or client recommendations
- [ ] Check whether partner review is required for AI-assisted deliverables
- [ ] Check whether a written AI policy exists
- [ ] Check whether approved tools are defined
- [ ] Check whether confidentiality rules mention AI use
- [ ] Check whether staff understand hallucination and calculation risk
- [ ] Identify highest-risk workflows for immediate restriction or review
