# AI Use Policy Template, Accounting Firms

## Purpose
This policy governs employee use of AI tools in accounting firm workflows while protecting client confidentiality, financial information, and work quality.

## Allowed uses
- drafting internal notes
- summarizing non-sensitive materials
- brainstorming communication drafts
- organizing internal workflows

## Prohibited uses
- entering taxpayer, payroll, banking, or confidential client financial data into unapproved tools
- using AI output as final tax, accounting, or advisory guidance without human review
- sending AI-generated client deliverables without reviewer approval

## Human review
All AI-assisted tax, advisory, payroll, and client-facing work must be reviewed by an assigned human reviewer before use.

## Ownership
The firm will assign a partner or manager to maintain the approved tools list, review exceptions, and coordinate updates.

## Note
This policy supports safe operations and does not replace legal, regulatory, or professional judgment.
