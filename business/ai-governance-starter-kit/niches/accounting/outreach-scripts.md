# Outreach Scripts, Accounting Firms

## Cold email
Subject: Quick question about AI use in your accounting firm

Hi {{First Name}},

Many accounting firms are starting to use tools like ChatGPT, Copilot, and Claude for drafting, summaries, and internal workflow support, but most still do not have clear rules for what staff can safely do with client or taxpayer information.

I help accounting firms put practical guardrails around AI use, including policy, approved tools guidance, and staff training.

If that is becoming relevant for your firm, I would be glad to share a simple overview.

## Call opener
I want to understand how AI is already showing up in your firm, where confidentiality or quality-control risks are highest, and whether a lightweight policy framework would help.
