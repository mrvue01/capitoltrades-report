# AI Governance Starter Kit

This kit is a practical starting point for launching an **AI Governance Setup for Small Businesses** service.

## Included files

1. `service-one-pager.md` - your offer summary
2. `client-questionnaire.md` - intake form for prospects and clients
3. `ai-risk-assessment-checklist.md` - delivery checklist for engagements
4. `ai-use-policy-template.md` - customizable policy template
5. `approved-tools-matrix-template.md` - tool approval and restrictions sheet
6. `training-deck-outline.md` - outline for your client training session
7. `landing-page-copy.md` - copy for a simple website or one-page site
8. `outreach-scripts.md` - email, LinkedIn, and follow-up scripts
9. `proposal-template.md` - a basic proposal you can send to prospects

## Recommended first offer

**Safe AI Use Setup for Small Businesses**

- Flat fee: **$999**
- Ideal client: **5 to 25 employees**
- Includes: risk assessment, AI use policy, approved tools matrix, staff training, and a 90-day rollout plan

Start with one niche first:

## Recommended client flow

1. Intro call
2. Send questionnaire
3. Review current AI usage
4. Complete risk checklist
5. Draft policy and tools matrix
6. Review with client
7. Deliver training
8. Offer quarterly retainer
