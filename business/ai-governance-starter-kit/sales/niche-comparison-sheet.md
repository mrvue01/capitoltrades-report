# Niche Comparison Sheet

## Best first niche by ease of sale
1. **Accounting firms**
2. **Insurance agencies**
3. **Law firms**
4. **Pharmacy**

## Why

### Accounting firms
- Easy to explain risk
- Clear confidentiality story
- High email and document workflow use
- Buyers often understand operational policy work

### Insurance agencies
- Strong volume of client communication
- Easy to frame around documentation and policyholder data
- Good fit for agencies already using AI informally

### Law firms
- High perceived need
- Strong value if sold well
- Slower trust-building and more scrutiny

### Pharmacy
- Strong risk case
- Higher compliance sensitivity
- Must avoid sounding like you are selling legal or HIPAA counsel without credentials

## Recommended go-to-market order
1. Accounting
2. Insurance
3. Law
4. Pharmacy

## Best niche for first revenue
**Accounting firms**

## Best niche for premium pricing later
**Law firms**

## Best niche for scalable outreach
**Insurance agencies**
