# Master Pricing Sheet, AI Governance Niche Offers

## Positioning principle
Price the offer as a **risk-reduction and operational-clarity package**, not as a generic policy document.

## Core package structure
### Starter
- discovery call
- AI risk assessment
- niche-specific AI use policy
- approved tools matrix
- staff training session
- 90-day rollout plan

### Standard
- everything in Starter
- deeper workflow review
- manager review rules
- customized department guidance
- second revision round

### Ongoing
- quarterly review retainer
- policy updates
- tool approval reviews
- refresher training
- manager Q&A

## Suggested pricing by niche

| Niche | Starter | Standard | Ongoing Monthly | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Accounting firms | $999 | $1,750 | $199 to $349 | Strong risk story around taxpayer and financial data |
| Law firms | $1,250 | $2,250 | $249 to $449 | Higher-perceived value, but requires careful positioning |
| Insurance agencies | $999 | $1,750 | $199 to $349 | Good fit for client communication and documentation risk |
| Pharmacy | $1,250 | $2,500 | $249 to $499 | Higher sensitivity, position carefully as operational guidance |

## Recommended first-offer pricing
If you are new and want traction fast:
- Accounting: **$999**
- Law firms: **$1,250**
- Insurance: **$999**
- Pharmacy: **$1,250**

## Discount guidance
Avoid discounting the core package heavily. Instead offer:
- a free 20-minute discovery call
- a bonus manager Q&A session
- a free first quarterly review if they sign ongoing support

## Upsells
- quarterly governance review
- staff refresher training
- new-tool approval workflow
- simple AI use manager handbook
- AI incident response checklist
