# Premium Positioning Guide

## Do not sell
- AI policy writing
- governance documentation
- AI consulting

## Sell this instead
- safe AI use setup
- AI risk and policy rollout
- practical AI guardrails for teams
- responsible AI operating framework for small businesses

## Messaging principles
1. Lead with risk reduction and clarity
2. Emphasize that you are not banning AI, you are making it usable and safer
3. Keep the offer productized and concrete
4. Use the buyer’s industry language
5. Avoid technical buzzwords unless the buyer uses them first

## Better headline examples
- Safe AI Use Setup for Accounting Firms
- Practical AI Guardrails for Law Firms
- AI Usage Rules and Review Framework for Insurance Agencies
- Operational AI Governance Setup for Pharmacy Teams

## Best proof points to emphasize
- faster adoption with less confusion
- better manager visibility
- reduced chance of sensitive data mishandling
- clearer human review expectations
- more consistency across staff

## Premium packaging tips
- use a named package
- include a defined timeline
- include staff training
- include a rollout plan
- add an optional retainer for continuity

## Premium close
"This is for teams that know AI is already showing up in the business and want a practical structure before informal use turns into preventable risk."
