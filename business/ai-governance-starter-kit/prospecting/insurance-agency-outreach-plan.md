# Insurance Agency Outreach Plan

## Best targets
- independent insurance agencies
- regional brokerages
- personal lines agencies
- commercial lines agencies

## Best contacts
- agency owner
- principal
- operations manager
- service manager

## Outreach angle
AI can speed up client communications and internal work, but agencies need rules for policyholder data, review requirements, and approved tools before usage gets sloppy.

## 30-day plan
- build list of 60 agencies
- send 10 to 15 personalized emails per weekday
- use LinkedIn to reach owners and principals
- follow up with a one-page overview
- aim for 5 to 7 discovery calls in month one

## Prospect list fields
- agency name
- website
- city
- decision maker
- email
- LinkedIn
- agency size estimate
- notes
- outreach date
- follow-up date
- status
