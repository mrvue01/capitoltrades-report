# Accounting Outreach Plan

## Best targets
- local CPA firms
- bookkeeping firms
- payroll and tax shops
- outsourced accounting providers

## Best contacts
- managing partner
- firm owner
- operations manager
- office manager

## Outreach angle
AI is already showing up in tax, bookkeeping, payroll, and advisory work. Firms need practical rules before staff start improvising with client and taxpayer data.

## 30-day plan
- build list of 50 firms
- send 10 personalized emails per weekday
- connect with 5 targets per day on LinkedIn
- follow up after 3 to 5 business days
- aim for 5 discovery calls in month one

## Prospect list fields
- firm name
- website
- city
- decision maker
- email
- LinkedIn
- size estimate
- notes
- outreach date
- follow-up date
- status
