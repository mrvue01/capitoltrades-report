# Pharmacy Outreach Plan

## Best targets
- independent pharmacies
- specialty pharmacies
- compounding pharmacies
- pharmacy management groups

## Best contacts
- owner
- operations leader
- pharmacy manager
- administrator

## Outreach angle
Pharmacy teams may be adopting AI informally for SOPs, training, and admin support, but they need careful guardrails around sensitive information and qualified review.

## 30-day plan
- build list of 30 to 40 targets
- personalize outreach carefully
- avoid hype and avoid promising compliance counsel
- position the service as operational AI governance support
- aim for 2 to 4 calls in month one

## Prospect list fields
- business name
- website
- city
- decision maker
- email
- LinkedIn
- type of pharmacy
- notes
- outreach date
- follow-up date
- status
