# Law Firm Outreach Plan

## Best targets
- boutique law firms
- estate planning firms
- business law firms
- family law firms
- personal injury firms

## Best contacts
- managing partner
- operations director
- office administrator
- innovation or practice support lead

## Outreach angle
Firms are experimenting with AI for drafting and productivity, but many still lack clear rules around confidentiality, privilege, and human review.

## 30-day plan
- build list of 40 firms
- send highly personalized outreach
- connect with practice leaders on LinkedIn
- follow up with a short overview, not a long pitch
- aim for 3 to 5 calls in month one

## Prospect list fields
- firm name
- practice area
- location
- decision maker
- email
- LinkedIn
- attorney count estimate
- notes
- outreach date
- follow-up date
- status
