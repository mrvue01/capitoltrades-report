# Training Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title
**Safe AI Use at [Company Name]**

## Slide 2: Why this matters
- AI tools are useful but not risk-free
- We want safe, productive, and consistent use
- This training explains what is allowed and what is not

## Slide 3: What AI tools are approved
- List approved tools
- Explain who approves new tools
- Explain that personal or unapproved tools are not allowed for company work unless approved

## Slide 4: What AI can help with
- drafting first versions
- summaries
- brainstorming
- internal productivity
- non-sensitive research support

## Slide 5: What AI cannot be used for
- entering sensitive client or employee data into unapproved tools
- making final decisions without human review
- sending unchecked content to clients
- using AI output as verified truth without checking it

## Slide 6: Data handling rules
- what counts as sensitive data
- what employees must never paste into unapproved systems
- what to do when unsure

## Slide 7: Human review rules
- every important output must be reviewed
- the employee remains responsible for the final result
- managers may require approval for certain workflows

## Slide 8: Common mistakes to avoid
- trusting hallucinated facts
- copying confidential information into public tools
- using AI-generated content without editing
- assuming AI output is compliant or client-ready

- acceptable use example
- risky use example
- prohibited use example

## Slide 10: Questions and escalation
- who owns AI governance internally
- where staff should ask questions
- how exceptions are handled

## Slide 11: Next steps
- read policy
- use only approved tools
- ask when unsure
- follow review rules
