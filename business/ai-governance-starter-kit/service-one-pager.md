# Service One-Pager

## Safe AI Use Setup for Small Businesses

Most small businesses are already using AI tools like ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, or Gemini, but very few have clear rules for safe use.

That creates real risk:
- client or company data may be pasted into public AI tools
- staff may rely on inaccurate AI output
- managers may not know what tools are being used
- different employees may follow different standards
- there may be no review process before AI-assisted content goes to clients

## What we do

We help small businesses create practical rules for safe AI use so their teams can benefit from AI without creating privacy, compliance, or operational problems.
