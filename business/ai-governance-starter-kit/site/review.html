Core starter kit

The business starts with one clear offer: Safe AI Use Setup for Small Businesses. These are the 9 core assets already built around that offer.

Asset 1

Service one-pager

Explains the offer, target buyer, risks solved, deliverables, and call to action in plain language.

Asset 2

Client questionnaire

Collects business basics, current AI usage, data exposure points, policy gaps, and decision-maker details.

Asset 3

AI risk checklist

Used during discovery and delivery to identify current tools, risky workflows, policy gaps, and rollout readiness.

Asset 4

AI use policy template

Your base policy covering approved tools, allowed uses, prohibited uses, data handling, and human review rules.

Asset 5

Approved tools matrix

A quick-reference sheet showing approved tools, allowed use cases, restricted use cases, prohibited data, and ownership.

Asset 6

Training deck outline

A practical training structure so policy adoption does not die in a PDF.

Asset 7

Landing page copy

Core website messaging for the offer, including problem, solution, who it is for, and starter pricing.

Asset 8

Outreach scripts

Cold email, LinkedIn outreach, follow-up language, and discovery call questions.

Asset 9

Proposal template

Simple scope, deliverables, timeline, price, responsibilities, and disclaimers for sending to prospects.

Sales and premium positioning

You asked for all three next items, so this now includes premium positioning, cross-niche pricing guidance, and niche-specific premium offer framing.

Positioning rules
✓
Do not sell “AI policy writing” or vague “AI consulting”.
✓
Sell safe AI use setup, practical guardrails, and AI risk-and-policy rollout.
✓
Lead with clarity, risk reduction, and manager visibility.
✓
Emphasize you are not banning AI, you are making it safer and usable.
